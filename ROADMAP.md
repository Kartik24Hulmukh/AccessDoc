## Ethical adoption gates
- [ ] 4/5 practitioners install and generate a sample in 15 minutes
- [ ] 4/5 accept a report from sanitized real-world evidence
- [ ] 3 deliver reports with no more than 30 minutes editing
- [ ] 2 repeat within 30 days; 1 external contribution
