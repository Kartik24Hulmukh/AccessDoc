What this report does—and does not—mean

Evidence, not certification

AccessDoc organizes supplied evidence. It does not establish WCAG, ADA, EAA, or legal compliance.

Automation has limits

Manual keyboard, assistive-technology, zoom, content-quality, and complete-process testing remain necessary.

Unknowns stay unknown

Source rules outside the reviewed catalog are never assigned guessed WCAG criteria.
