## Gate criteria

- [ ] ≥5 practitioners have provided feedback
- [ ] ≥2 practitioners answered "Yes" to "Would you hand the output to a client?"
- [ ] No critical security issues reported by practitioners
- [ ] No conformance-claim concerns raised by practitioners

## How to use

1. Identify 5+ accessibility practitioners (auditors, consultants, in-house a11y leads)
2. Send one of the outreach email variants from `outreach-emails.md`
3. Track each contact in this sheet
4. When they respond, record their feedback and Y/N answer
5. Do NOT proceed to broad launch until the gate criteria are met
6. If a practitioner says "No, I wouldn't hand this to a client," record WHY — that's the most valuable feedback
