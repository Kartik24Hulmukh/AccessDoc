# Governance

AccessDoc uses a maintainer-led, consensus-seeking model. Maintainers own releases, security response, roadmap prioritization, and policy enforcement.

- Routine changes: one independent approving maintainer and passing required checks.
- High-risk security, privacy, mapping, report-claim, workflow, or release-policy changes: two qualified approvals when two eligible maintainers exist.
- Authors cannot be the sole approver.
- Unresolved security, privacy, accessibility, or misleading-claim concerns block merge.
- Governance changes use public pull requests.
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed; affected maintainers recuse.
- Contributor → reviewer → maintainer progression is based on sustained, high-quality, trustworthy work.

Until real maintainers are confirmed, roles remain vacant in `MAINTAINERS.md`; no foundation, committee, or team is implied.
