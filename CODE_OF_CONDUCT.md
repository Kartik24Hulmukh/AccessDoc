# Code of Conduct

AccessDoc adopts the Contributor Covenant 2.1 principles. Be respectful, inclusive, constructive, and considerate. Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption, and publishing private information are unacceptable across issues, pull requests, discussions, events, and project representation.

Maintainers may correct, warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove participation proportionate to impact. Report conduct privately to a confirmed maintainer listed by the repository; no address is invented here. Enforcement must respect reporter privacy and avoid retaliation.

Canonical policy: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
